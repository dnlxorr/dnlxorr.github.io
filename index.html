Profile

Physicist and self-taught software developer. Along all of my formal education, I've always found the way to dedicate an important part of my time to the development of software both, for scientific and nonscientific purposes. I have 7+ years of experience in python and c++ programming computer simulations of physical systems. I am fully capable of running simulations of complex systems such as those found in quantum computing. My most recent experience accounts for 5 years as Android developer. Nowadays, I'm fully immersed in multiplatform development to improve academic processes for teachers and students at the university and school levels through automation and data management.
